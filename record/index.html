    🎙️ RECORD

    The Record feature allows you to capture audio live with real-time control over effects like pitch, bass, echo, reverb, and more. It’s designed to let you shape your voice or sound from the very beginning—perfect for podcasters, voice artists, and creators.

    🎤 Pro Tip: Record in a quiet environment with a good microphone to ensure clean and crisp audio.

    🎚️ Real-Time Controls While Recording

    You can adjust these live during recording: - Bass - Treble - Pitch - Reverb - Echo - Volume - Fade - Tempo

    These controls work just like in Advanced Edit, but in real-time.

    🔧 How to Use Record:

    1. Tap the "Record" button on the main screen.

    2. A recording panel opens—click Start Recording 🟥 to begin.

    3. During recording:

    4. Use Pause Recording ⏸️ to temporarily stop.
    5. Tap Resume Recording ▶️ to continue from where you left off.
    6. During recording, you can adjust the sensitivity in decibels to reduce background noise and prevent the capture of unwanted sounds.

    7. Once you're done, tap Stop ⏹️ to finish recording.

    8. After stopping, you’ll be taken to the Advanced Edit screen where you can:

    9. Apply all pro-level effects (bass, pitch, tempo, etc.).
    10. Tweak your recorded audio the same way you would edit an uploaded file.
    11. Once editing is complete, tap the Save button (📥 on the top-right corner).
    12. Choose your desired file name and audio format, then tap Save to export your final audio file.

    🎧 This seamless workflow lets you: - Capture → Preview → Edit → Save
    All in one smooth and powerful flow.

    ✅ Tip: You can pause and resume as many times as needed to create multi-part recordings in a single file.
