# 🗣️ VOICE CHANGER

## 📖 Voice Changer Description

A **Voice Changer** is a powerful audio effect that allows you to **modify the pitch** of a voice, making it sound **bolder or leaner** by adjusting its frequency. 🎶

- **Lowering the pitch** results in a **deeper, bolder** voice — ideal for dramatic, intense, or authoritative tones.
- **Raising the pitch** creates a **lighter, leaner** voice — perfect for playful, cartoonish, or child-like effects.

This effect works by modifying the **frequency and timing** of the voice waveform. Whether you're adding character, emotion, or fun to your audio, the Voice Changer makes it easy to transform voices creatively. 🔄🔊

### 🎚️ Voice Changer Knob – User Guide

#### Steps:
1. Import or open the **audio file** you want to edit.
2. Use the **single knob** on the screen to adjust pitch:
   - Turn **counterclockwise** to **lower the pitch** and create a **bolder, deeper** voice (minimum: `0.7`).
   - Turn **clockwise** to **raise the pitch** and produce a **leaner, lighter** voice (maximum: `1.8`).
3. Apply **small, gradual changes** to avoid distorted or unnatural results.
4. **Preview** the audio after each adjustment to fine-tune the pitch to your liking.
5. Once you're happy with the sound:
   - Click the **Save** button at the **top right** of the screen.
6. Choose your **output format** (e.g., MP3, WAV) and **rename the file** if needed.
7. Click the **Save** button at the **bottom** to **export** your edited audio.

---

✅ **Tip:** Lower pitch works great for villain voices or narration. Higher pitch adds fun and comedy to your audio — try both and see what fits your style!
