# 🗑️ DATA DELETION REQUEST

## 📖 Delete Your App Data

We respect your privacy and give users full control over their data. 🔐

If you would like to request deletion of your account data, recordings, analytics data, or any stored information related to the app, follow the steps below.

Once the request is processed, all associated user data will be permanently removed from our systems. ⚠️

---

## 📱 Steps to Request Data Deletion

1. Open the app settings screen.
2. Navigate to the **Privacy & Data** section.
3. Tap on **Delete My Data**.
4. Confirm the deletion request.
5. Your request will be submitted successfully. ✅

## ⚠️ Important Information

- Data deletion is **permanent** and cannot be undone.
- Deleted recordings and exported files cannot be recovered.
- Some temporary backup data may take a few days to fully disappear from servers.
- Analytics data collected anonymously may remain in aggregated form without identifying users.

---

## 📧 Need Help?

If you face any issues while deleting your data, contact us through the support email provided in the app store listing.

---

✅ **Tip:** Before deleting your data, export or back up any important recordings you wish to keep.
