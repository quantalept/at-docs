# ⭐ Upgrade to Pro

## Why Go Pro?

To unlock powerful features like **Karaoke**, advanced **audio editing tools**, and the **Voice Changer**, you’ll need to upgrade to **Pro** — a one-time or yearly paid plan designed for users who want the full audio experience.

---

## 💰 How to Upgrade:

1. On the **home screen**, tap the **money icon** located at the **top right corner**.
2. This will take you to the **"Upgrade to Pro"** page.
3. You'll see a list of **exclusive features** unlocked by the Pro version.
4. At the bottom, you’ll find two purchase options:
   - **1 Year Pro** – ₹50
   - **Lifetime Pro** – ₹200
5. Select your preferred plan.
6. Proceed with the **Google Play payment** popup that appears.
7. Once the payment is successful, the **Pro features** will be automatically unlocked.

## 🔁 Restore Purchase (Cross-Device Access)

If you've already purchased Pro and want to access it on another device:

- Just tap the **"Restore Purchase"** button on the **Upgrade to Pro** screen.
- Make sure you're logged in with the **same Google account** used for the original purchase.
- This will instantly unlock Pro features on the new device — no need to pay again.
