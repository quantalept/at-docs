# 🎙️ RECORD

The **Record** feature allows you to capture audio live with real-time control over effects like pitch, bass, echo, reverb, and more. It’s designed to let you shape your voice or sound from the very beginning—perfect for podcasters, voice artists, and creators.

🎤 **Pro Tip**: Record in a quiet environment with a good microphone to ensure clean and crisp audio.

---

## 🎚️ Real-Time Controls While Recording

You can adjust these live during recording:
- **Bass**
- **Treble**
- **Pitch**
- **Reverb**
- **Echo**
- **Volume**
- **Fade**
- **Tempo**

These controls work **just like in Advanced Edit**, but in **real-time**.

---

## 🔧 How to Use Record:

1. Tap the **"Record"** button on the main screen.

2. A recording panel opens—click **Start Recording** 🟥 to begin.
   <div style="display: flex; gap: 20px; margin: 10px 0;">
      <img src="/assets/01Record.jpeg" alt="Record Button" width="200"/>
      <img src="/assets/02Record.jpeg" alt="Recording Panel" width="200"/>
      <img src="/assets/03Record.jpeg" alt="Record Pause" width="200"/>
   </div>

3. During recording:
   - Use **Pause Recording** ⏸️ to temporarily stop.
   - Tap **Resume Recording** ▶️ to continue from where you left off.
   - During recording, you can adjust the sensitivity in decibels to reduce background noise and prevent the capture of unwanted sounds. 
4. Once you're done, tap **Stop** ⏹️ to finish recording.
   <div style="display: flex; gap: 20px; margin: 10px 0;">
      <img src="/assets/04Record.jpeg" alt="Record Resume" width="200"/>
      <img src="/assets/05Record.jpeg" alt="Record Stop" width="200"/>
      <img src="/assets/06Record.jpeg" alt="Editing recorded" width="200"/>
   </div>
5. After stopping, you’ll be taken to the **Advanced Edit** screen where you can:
   - Apply all pro-level effects (bass, pitch, tempo, etc.).
   - Tweak your recorded audio the same way you would edit an uploaded file.
6. Once editing is complete, tap the **Save** button (📥 on the top-right corner).
7. Choose your **desired file name** and **audio format**, then tap **Save** to export your final audio file.

🎧 This seamless workflow lets you:
- Capture → Preview → Edit → **Save**  
All in one smooth and powerful flow.

---

✅ **Tip**: You can pause and resume as many times as needed to create multi-part recordings in a single file.
