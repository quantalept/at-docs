# 🔄 REVERSAL

The **reverse effect** is an audio transformation where the selected portion of the sound is played **backwards**, creating an unusual and often surreal effect. This process inverts the waveform so that it plays in reverse order, turning even familiar sounds into something eerie, dreamy, or otherworldly.

🎧 **Use cases**:
- Add suspense or mystery in background scores
- Make transitions more creative in remixes
- Reveal Easter eggs or hidden phrases (a trick often used in old-school music production!)

Reversing a vocal line, instrument stroke, or ambient sound can surprise the listener, often catching attention with its haunting or psychedelic tone.

### 🛠️ User Guide:

1. **Select** the audio file from your library.
2. **Trim** the section you want to reverse using the **start and end markers** (if you want only a portion reversed).
3. Click **Save** and enter a title (optional).
4. Your reversed audio will be processed and saved automatically.

📝 **Note**: Only the selected trimmed portion will be reversed—not the entire file—unless you choose the whole duration.
