# 🎤 KARAOKE

## 📖 Karaoke Description

A **Karaoke music extractor** is a tool designed to **separate vocals from the instrumental** (music) track of a song. This process, known as **vocal isolation** or **vocal removal**, allows users to generate karaoke-ready versions of songs or remix audio content creatively.

While perfect vocal extraction can be challenging due to overlapping frequencies and recording complexities, modern tools use **advanced machine learning models** to achieve impressively accurate results. These models are trained on large datasets and apply techniques such as:

- **Center channel extraction**
- **Phase inversion**
- **Frequency-based filtering**

Together, these methods help isolate and remove vocal elements, leaving behind a clean instrumental backing track.

### 🎚️ Karaoke – User Guide



#### Steps:
1. **Select the music track** you want to convert into a karaoke version.
2. Check your available **Karaoke credits** (displayed at the **top-right corner** of the screen).
   - 1 credit is required for every **5-minute song**.
   - The credit counter may take a few seconds to update after track selection.
   <div style="display: flex; gap: 20px; margin: 10px 0;">
      <img src="/assets/01karaoke.jpeg" alt="Karaoke Button" width="200"/>
      <img src="/assets/02karaoke.jpeg" alt="Karaoke  Screen" width="200"/>
   </div>
3. If you have **insufficient credits**:
   - Click the **Credits button**.
   - You’ll be redirected to the **Karaoke Upgrade** page.
   - Choose to add either **5 credits** or **10 credits** as per your need.
4. Once credits are available, click the **Save** button to proceed.
   <div style="display: flex; gap: 20px; margin: 10px 0;">
      <img src="/assets/03karaoke.jpeg" alt="Karaoke Save" width="200"/>
      <img src="/assets/04karaoke.jpeg" alt="Karaoke  Processing" width="200"/>
   </div>
5. The file will now:
   - Get **compressed**, 
   - Be **uploaded** to the server,
   - Undergo **vocal separation**, and
   - Be **converted** to a karaoke version.
6. Please wait patiently — this process may take some time depending on the file size and network.
7. Once complete, the **karaoke file will be automatically downloaded** to your local storage.

---

✅ **Note:** Karaoke extraction quality may vary depending on the complexity of the original mix. For best results, use high-quality audio files.
