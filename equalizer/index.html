    🎛️ ADVANCED EDIT

    Advanced Edit allows powerful post-processing of uploaded audio files. These controls help you refine the sonic character, clarity, and depth of existing tracks.

    🎧 Tip: Always preview each change. Use headphones for precise sound adjustments.

    a. Bass

    Bass enhances the low-end frequencies (20 Hz – 250 Hz), adding depth and warmth.

    • Boost sub-bass for impact (EDM, Hip-Hop).
    • Tame mid-bass to avoid muddiness.

    🔧 How to Use:

    1. Turn the knob clockwise to increase richness.
    2. Turn counterclockwise to reduce low-end boom.

    b. Treble

    Treble manages high-end frequencies (2 kHz – 20 kHz), adding sparkle and clarity.

    🔧 How to Use:

    1. Turn the knob right to brighten vocals and strings.
    2. Turn it left to smooth harsh or piercing sounds.

    c. Volume

    Volume sets the overall loudness.

    🔧 How to Use:

    1. Rotate right to boost.
    2. Rotate left to reduce.
    3. Find a comfortable listening level that avoids clipping.

    d. Pitch

    Pitch adjusts how high or low the voice sounds.

    • Raise pitch = chipmunk/sharp.
    • Lower pitch = deep/bold.

    🔧 How to Use:

    1. Rotate knob right to raise pitch (up to 1.8x).
    2. Rotate knob left to lower pitch (down to 0.7x).

    e. Tempo

    Tempo changes audio speed (not pitch).

    • Slower = calm, soft.
    • Faster = upbeat, energetic.

    🔧 How to Use:

    1. Turn left for slow-motion.
    2. Turn right to speed things up.

    f. Echo

    Echo adds reflective tail to simulate bounce and space.

    🔧 How to Use:

    1. Rotate right to add dreamy ambiance.
    2. Rotate left to minimize echo.

    🚨 Avoid overuse—can cause muddiness.

    g. Fade

    Fade In/Out helps smooth transitions at start or end.

    🔧 How to Use:

    1. Tap Fade icon near waveform.
    2. Choose: Fade In, Fade Out, or Both.
    3. Drag handles to set fade length.

    h. Reverb

    Reverb simulates audio in physical spaces (like rooms or halls).

    Presets:

    • 🏟️ Amphitheatre
    • 🏫 Classroom
    • ⛪ Cathedral
    • 🏛️ Large Church
    • 🚿 Bathroom
    • 🏰 Minster
    • 🌀 Slinky

    🔧 How to Use:

    1. Select preset.
    2. Use the knob to adjust the intensity. If set to 0, no changes will occur.
    3. Preview carefully—too much drowns detail.
